Adaptive Reuse: Co-working Space

This project transformed a vacant factory into a dynamic co-working environment by designing an adaptive modular platform. Recycled materials and digital fabrication techniques were utilized to create modular partitions, tables, and chairs.

Project Type: Final Project, University of Art

Location: Vacant Mashhad Coca-Cola Factory, Mashhad, Iran

Date: February 2022
