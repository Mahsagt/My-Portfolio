Adapting a Historical Space for Educational Use

The goal of this project is to address the challenges of repurposing a historical building for educational purposes. Key challenges include adapting the high ceilings and long, brick-covered corridors to create a more varied and functional space. The design seeks to preserve the historical character while introducing elements that enhance the building's suitability for modern educational needs.
