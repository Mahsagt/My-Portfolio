Architectural Sketches

In this section, I present a collection of architectural sketches and hand drawings that illustrate how conceptual ideas are transformed into tangible designs. These works demonstrate the consideration of various climates—such as cold, humid, and warm environments—and how these conditions influence the building's form and the relationships within the plan. The attempt is to integrate climate-responsive design principles into the architectural process.
