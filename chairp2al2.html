Chair Design for the 2021 Seat Prize

This chair was designed to accommodate both single and multiple users. By reconfiguring its structure and rotating its components, it can transform from a single seat into a table with stools. Created for the 2021 Seat Prize competition in Isfahan, Iran, this design reached the final stage and was showcased as a finalist at the Safavi House exhibition.
