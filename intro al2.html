About Me

I am Mahsa Ghanad Torshizi, with a Bachelor’s in Architectural Engineering and a Master’s in Interior Architecture, where I focused on sustainable design principles and occupant well-being. My passion lies in exploring how environments can balance human needs with environmental responsibility. I have worked extensively on adaptive reuse projects, integrating responsive architecture with sensor and actuator technology, and designing modular, flexible objects that support sustainable, user-centered spaces. I am dedicated to advancing the interaction between people and buildings to enhance both functionality and satisfaction.
